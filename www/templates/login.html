Our Goal

Wink connect's people through location based posting

Understanding The Thread

Posts within X miles of your location will appear in your feed. You may Accept posts that you think may pertain to you as well as Tag your friends in posts that fit their description

Accepting Posts

Accepting posts will allow the original author of the post to see some of your information to help them decide if you are actually the person they posted about. This allows original poster to find potential matches, but keeps their information private until they think they have found the person they posted about.
